unfuzz: solutions for culture

We are a public organisation aiming to help the cultural sector build dialogue with visitors and develop them as active, critical-thinking participants.

Founded in 2023, Unfuzz works at the intersection of cultural education and social inclusion. We focus on culture as a mediator in society, follow global developments in culture and research, and stay sensitive to local needs — open to innovation and international practice.

We develop cultural mediation capacity, deliver learning programmes for institutions and teams, and design digital solutions for museums and cultural organisations.
