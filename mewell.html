About the project

Unfuzz, together with international partners from Austria and Malta, is implementing the Erasmus+ Small-Scale Partnership in Adult Education project MeWell.

The goal of the project is to strengthen the competencies of cultural sector educators and andragogues by applying cultural mediation methods. These methods foster more active cultural and social engagement among adults aged 50+ and seniors, particularly those whose opportunities to access cultural experiences are limited.

Who it is for
Primary audience Adults aged 50+ and seniors, including those with fewer opportunities.
Professional audience Cultural educators, andragogues, and non-formal education providers in the social sector.
Outcomes

1) Guidelines (methodological resource)

A methodological resource designed for non-formal education providers in the cultural and social sectors who work with adults aged 50+ and seniors, including those with fewer opportunities. The guidelines support the development of cultural and art mediation skills: not only presenting artworks and information, but also engaging participants in discussion, encouraging reflection, and creating meaningful experiences.

2) Cultural mediation e-platform

A digital space hosting training materials and enabling the exchange of practices and knowledge among cultural mediation practitioners. The platform will be regularly updated with new methods and examples of good practice.
